As you can see, the data contains information on the country the wine is from, as well as the province, the macro-level region (which is below the province) and the micro-level region_2 (which is below the region_1). The designation represents the part of the vinyard where the vintner claims the wine comes from. The price is the price (in dollars) of the wine. The points are the rating of the wine, given by WineMags judges. Finally, the variety reflects the grape (or grapes) used to make the wine, and the winery reflects the name of the winery that grew the wine.
