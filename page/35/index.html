The Normalized Mass Moment of Inertia proposed by Li et al (2013) is deceptively hard to express. Given some shape and a distribution of attributes around the shape, they state the NMMI ratio in Fan et al (forthcoming) as: $$NMMI = \frac{I_O^’} {I^{G_i}_{i_mass}}$$ where $I$ represents the area-standardized mass moment of inertia.
