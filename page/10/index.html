An illumination of Dan Drezner’s new book, the Ideas Industry, reviewer David Sessions outlines Drezner’s main arguments and connects them back to a Gramscian perspective on the social responsibility of the intellectual. In doing so, Sessions sees Drezner extending and furthering Jacoby’s concerns about the lunchpail academic & professionalization of academic work into the construction of an entirely new “private” intellectual.
